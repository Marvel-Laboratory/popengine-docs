# Installation

Last Second Coupon is installed directly from the Shopware Store, just like any other Shopware 6 app.

## Step-by-Step Installation

1. Log in to your **Shopware 6 Administration** panel
2. Navigate to **Extensions > Store**
3. Search for **"Last Second Coupon"**
4. Click **Add extension** and confirm the installation
5. Once installed, go to **Extensions > My extensions**
6. Find **Last Second Coupon** in the list and click **Install**
7. After installation completes, click **Activate**

![Installing from the Shopware Store](/img/admin/installation-store.png)

:::tip
Make sure your Shopware 6 version meets the [system requirements](/docs/installation/requirements) before installing.
:::

## After Installation

Once activated, Last Second Coupon will:

- Appear in your Administration sidebar under **Extensions**
- Automatically create a default popup template
- Set up sensible default discount settings (10% off, 15-minute timer)

You can access the app by clicking **Last Second Coupon** in the Administration menu.

## Updating the App

When a new version is available:

1. Go to **Extensions > My extensions**
2. You will see an **Update** button next to Last Second Coupon
3. Click **Update** and wait for it to complete
4. Clear your browser cache if the admin interface looks outdated

:::info
Updates are applied automatically without losing your popup designs or coupon settings. All existing data is preserved.
:::

## Uninstalling

If you need to remove the app:

1. Go to **Extensions > My extensions**
2. Click the **three-dot menu** next to Last Second Coupon
3. Select **Deactivate**, then **Uninstall**

:::caution
Uninstalling the app will remove all popup designs, coupon settings, and analytics data. This action cannot be undone.
:::
