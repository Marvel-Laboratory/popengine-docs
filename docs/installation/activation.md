# Activation & License

After installing Last Second Coupon, you need to activate it to start using all features.

## Activating the App

1. Go to **Extensions > My extensions** in your Shopware Administration
2. Find **Last Second Coupon** in the list
3. Click the **Activate** toggle switch
4. The app will register with our servers and set up your shop automatically

![Activating the app](/img/admin/activation-toggle.png)

:::info
Activation happens automatically through the Shopware app system. No license key is required — your Shopware Store purchase is your license.
:::

## What Happens During Activation

When you activate the app, the following happens behind the scenes:

- Your shop is registered with the Last Second Coupon service
- A default popup design is created for you
- Default coupon settings are configured (10% discount, 15-minute expiration)
- The storefront widget script is prepared for your sales channels

## Verifying Activation

To confirm the app is working:

1. Open the **Last Second Coupon** section in your Administration
2. You should see the **Popup Manager** dashboard
3. A default popup should already be listed
4. Visit your storefront in a new browser tab and try triggering the exit-intent popup (move your mouse toward the browser's close button)
