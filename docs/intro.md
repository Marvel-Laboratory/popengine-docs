# Getting Started

Last Second Coupon helps you recover abandoned carts in your Shopware 6 store by showing time-limited discount popups to customers who are about to leave your checkout.

When a visitor shows signs of leaving — like moving their mouse toward the browser's close button — a customizable popup appears offering a limited-time discount. The urgency of a countdown timer encourages them to complete their purchase right away.

## What You Can Do

- **Design custom popups** using a drag-and-drop editor with ready-made templates
- **Set discount rules** including percentage or fixed discounts, minimum cart values, and usage limits
- **Target specific pages** to show popups only on certain pages, categories, or products
- **Capture emails** by optionally requiring an email before revealing the coupon code
- **Track performance** with built-in analytics showing coupon usage and redemption rates
- **Target by country** to show different popups to visitors from different regions
- **Support multiple languages** with English and German popup content

## Quick Start

1. [Install the app](/docs/installation/installation) from the Shopware Store
2. [Activate your license](/docs/installation/activation)
3. [Create your first popup](/docs/admin/popup-manager) using a template
4. [Configure your discount](/docs/admin/coupon-settings) rules
5. Your popup is live — check [Analytics](/docs/admin/analytics) to track results
